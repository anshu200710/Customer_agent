PURPOSE OF CALL



Purpose of the AI Voice Call System
The primary goal of this system is to automate the JCB Service Desk, ensuring that every customer complaint is recorded accurately and instantly without needing a human agent to answer every call.

24/7 Availability: Customers can register complaints at any time (nights, holidays, or weekends).
Instant Machine Verification: The AI instantly checks the machine number against your database to identify the owner and model, reducing manual data entry errors.
Structured Data Collection: It ensures every complaint has a valid machine number, a clear problem description, a verified phone number, and a specific location.
Operational Efficiency: By the time a service manager looks at the dashboard, the complaint is already logged with all the necessary details, allowing the technician to be dispatched immediately.
🛠️ How to Proceed (The Workflow)
To get the most out of this system, here is the standard procedure for a customer call:

Step 1: The Initial Greeting
The customer calls the service number. The AI welcomes them professionally: "Namaskar. JCB service center mein aapka swagat hai..."

Step 2: Machine Identification
Customer Action: The user says their machine number (e.g., "Mera machine number 33 05447 hai").
System Action: The AI cleans the audio, extracts the digits, and fetches the customer’s name and machine details from the database.
Step 3: Complaint Recording
Customer Action: The user explains the issue (e.g., "Engine start nahi ho raha").
System Action: The AI records this as the complaint_details.
Step 4: Detail Verification
Customer Action: The user confirms if the calling number is correct or provides a new one. They also state their city or branch.
System Action: The AI uses "Intent Detection" to understand if the user wants to Confirm or Change their details, ensuring the contact info is 100% accurate.
Step 5: Final Submission
Customer Action: The user hears a summary and says "Haan, submit kar do" (Yes, submit it).
System Action: The AI sends the full data package to the JCB Complaint API. The call ends with a polite thank-you message.
📈 Business Outcome
When the call ends, your backend system automatically receives a completed complaint entry in the same format as a manual entry, including Machine Model, Customer Name, and job location, ready for your service team to take action.
